About our team

Aarik Googe - ceo // lead photographer

I'm Aarik Googe, CEO and lead Toeless photographer and film maker. My intrest in photography sprouted at a young age while film came later. My goal for toeless is to be world renown as an artist and entrepreneur.


Julian Baker - coo // Lead web designer

I'm Julian Baker, head of web design on the Toeless team. I've been interested in code technology since 2010, and my interest has only grown since then. I've coded games, websites, addons, chrome extensions and more. Most of my code repositories are publicly hosted on my Github. My goal with Toeless is to expand to new heights and prove myself to the world.


Max Hanke - Lead creative designer

I'm Max Hanke, Lead Creative designer of toeless. I design as a hobby and I thoroughly enjoy pizza, painting, and film making with the team. I've been interested in design from the jump and im happy to be working on the Toeless team.


Sydney Berman - On Set Stylist

I’m Sydney Berman, onset stylist for the Toeless team. The fashion and modeling side of photography has always been something that I’ve had an interest in. With Toeless, I’ve been able to explore working with the models to help create interesting and authentic photos.
